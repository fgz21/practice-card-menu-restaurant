Instructions

  1. Crack 2-3 large eggs into a bowl and whisk them together. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Once the butter has melted or the oil is hot, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Let the eggs cook until the edges start to set. If using an optional filling, add it to one half of the omelette.
  5. Use a spatula to gently fold the other half of the omelette over the filling.
  6. Cook for another minute, until the eggs are almost set and the filling is heated through.
  7. Slide the omelette out of the pan onto a plate and serve hot.
